# Product Principles — Index

> **Status: AUTHORITATIVE.** The permanent home for **Product Principles** — long-lived product requirements
> that guide future development. Product Principles are **not architecture** and **not implementation**: they
> do not describe how the system is built or coded; they state enduring product truths every future feature
> must satisfy. They sit under the Constitution (`ALH_PRODUCT_PHILOSOPHY.md`) and complement — but do not
> restate — the Product Canon (`ACTIVLIFE_HUB_PRODUCT_CANON.md`).

## What a Product Principle is

- A **long-lived product requirement**, expected to remain true across many implementation stages.
- **Product-level**, not technical: no schemas, APIs, or code.
- A **test** future work must pass, not a task list.

When a decision conflicts with a Product Principle, the principle wins unless it is deliberately and
explicitly amended.

## Current Product Principles

- **`AI_PROJECT_MODELING_PRINCIPLE.md`** — ActivLife Hub progressively transforms an idea into an executable
  digital model of a future event; every stage enriches the same model. ALH is not an AI text generator and
  not a checklist builder.
- **`PROFESSIONAL_BASELINE_PRINCIPLE.md`** — ActivLife Hub must provide the complete operational baseline a
  professional organizer expects (a replacement for operational software, not an additional app); the AI is
  built on top of that baseline, never instead of it.

*New Product Principles are added here as they are discovered and accepted.*
